Safety guidelines: All checks must be lawful, decent, and safe. No confrontation, no trespassing, no exceptions.

What to include when you email

  1. Hotel name and full address.
  2. Date and approximate time you visited.
  3. What you observed (concise, factual), and how you verified it.
  4. Links to any public sources (if applicable).

  • Graphic Designers — branding, logo, design.
